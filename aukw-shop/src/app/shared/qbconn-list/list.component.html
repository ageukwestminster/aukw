Quickbooks Connections

Quickbooks is the software we use to account for our profit and loss. A connection is a link between an App (like this website) and the Quickbooks online system.

Only the QB Company Admin can make a connection between an app and a company file. An App can only have one connection per company file and we have two company files so there are two connections.
